# About Us

Welcome to HIIT Hustle, where your fitness adventure takes new heights! At HIIT Hustle, we believe that high-intensity interval training not only transforms your body, but also empowers your life. We're here to change the way you train by bringing the power and thrill of HIIT right to your fingertips via our unique online application.

## Our Mission

Our objective is straightforward: to make high-quality HIIT exercises accessible, effective, and entertaining for everyone. Whether you're a busy professional with limited free time, a fitness fanatic looking for a new challenge, or a newbie looking to prioritise your health, HIIT Hustle is for you.

# We Offer

  • ## Custom Workouts

    Our application offers a fully customizable workout interface to bring the joy of exercising to everyone. You can create distinctive workouts by also editing the rest time between each exercise individually to achieve better performance and find your own pace!

  • ## Dynamic User Interface

    With the dynamic interface, our users can track the duration of each exercise and the total workout time while gaining deeper insights into the exercise movements through GIFs and instructions.

  • ## Workout Tracker

    Our app monitors your workout sessions to better understand your preferences and displays your progress each time you successfully complete a workout.

  • ## Theme Color Setting

    With multiple theme color selection options, our app enhances your experience by allowing you to personalize the interface. You can set the theme color in the app settings!

  • ## Mobile Compatible

    Our app is mobile-friendly and operates without any performance issues, allowing users to enjoy HIIT workouts anywhere, anytime. Moreover, its intuitive design ensures a seamless and engaging user experience on any device.
